SPIDER-MAN: HERO OR MENACE?

By J. Jonah Jameson, Editor-in-Chief

Once again, the so-called “friendly neighborhood Spider-Man” was spotted swinging through the city late last night, chasing down what appeared to be a group of heavily armed criminals. But witnesses report significant property damage in the aftermath of the encounter.

“There were webs everywhere!” said one disgruntled New Yorker. “My car’s completely stuck to a lamppost!”

The question remains: Is Spider-Man truly helping this city, or is he causing more chaos than he prevents? The Bugle demands answers. We urge the authorities to unmask this vigilante menace before someone really gets hurt.
